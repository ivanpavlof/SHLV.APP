01

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SHLV (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your data.

The App is operated by an individual developer based in Bulgaria.

02

Data we collect

2.1 — Account data

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Sign-in provider

2.2 — Subscription data

We store:

  • Subscription status
  • Platform purchase token

This data is stored in our backend via Supabase.

2.3 — User content

  • Inventory data — items, locations, images — is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not store this data on our servers.

2.4 — AI processing data

When you use Pro features:

  • Images may be sent to Supabase Edge Functions and then to OpenAI for analysis.
  • Item data — names, tags, descriptions — may be sent for embedding and categorization.

We do not store this content on our servers.

03

How we use data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the App.
  • Enable Pro features.
  • Improve AI functionality and performance.
04

Third-party services

We use the following third-party service providers ("processors"):

  • Supabase — authentication, database, backend logic.
  • OpenAI — AI processing (image and text analysis).

These providers may process and temporarily store data according to their own policies.

05

Data retention

  • Account and subscription data is retained while your account is active.
  • AI-related data may be temporarily retained by third-party providers.
  • We do not store your inventory data on our servers.
06

Legal basis

Under the GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Performance of a contract — providing the App.
  • Legitimate interest — improving the service.
07

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.

You may delete your account at any time using the in-app feature.

When you delete your account: your account data is deleted from Supabase. Some data may remain temporarily in third-party logs.
08

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

09

Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13.

10

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App means you accept the changes.
